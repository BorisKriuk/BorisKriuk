## About

I work at the intersection of machine learning, deep learning, and data-driven engineering. My research and engineering focus is on building systems where learned models and analytical physics co-exist productively — generative design, scientific machine learning, applied optimization, and interpretable architectures for high-stakes domains.

This profile is a live record of my open work: the projects I ship, the experiments I run, and the ideas I am willing to expose to public review.

## Working principles

- **Reproducibility first.** Every experiment ships with a seed, a config, and a clear path to re-run it.
- **Interpretability over opacity.** A model that can be inspected and reasoned about is worth more than one that simply scores well.
- **Analytical baselines matter.** Before invoking a deep model, I write the closed-form solution. Often it wins.
- **Ship the UI.** Research that nobody can run is research nobody will use. I build interactive front-ends for my models.
